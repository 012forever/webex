HTML

The body of a document contains the document's content.
The content may be presented by a user agent in a variety of ways.
For example, for visual browsers,
you can think of the body as a canvas where the content appears:
text, images, colors, graphics, etc. For audio user agents,
the same content may be spoken.
Since style sheets are now the preferred way to specify a document's presentation,
the presentational attributes of BODY have been deprecated.
